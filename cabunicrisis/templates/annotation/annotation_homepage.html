Welcome to Functional Annotation

The functional annotation step in our pipeline takes in fna, faa, multi-fasta, and gff files to create annotated gff files for Comparative Genomics.

We approach this through homology- and ab initio-based tools. Inputs to homology are clustered beforehand.

Clustering: CD-HIT. Homology: EGGNOG, CARD, VFDB. Ab initio: TMHMM2, SignalP, and PilerCR.


Unfortunately, because we need to ensure that the inputs to this step are paired, the outputs of Gene Prediction are immediately fed into Functional Annotation.
