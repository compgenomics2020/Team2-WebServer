Welcome to Functional Annotation

The functional annotation step in our pipeline takes in fna, faa, multi-fasta, and gff files to create annotated gff files for Comparative Genomics. We approach this through homology- and ab initio-based tools. Inputs to homology are clustered beforehand.

Clustering: CD-HIT. Homology: EGGNOG and VFDB. Ab initio: TMHMM2 and SignalP.


Please enter faa, fna, and gff files with the same naming schemes. There must be an equal number of each.
